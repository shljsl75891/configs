---
name: grill-me
description: Grill the user on raw requirements, new ideas, plans, or designs to resolve ambiguity before implementation.
---

Interview me relentlessly about every aspect of this until we reach shared understanding. Walk each branch of the decision tree, resolving dependencies between decisions one-by-one. For each question, lead with your recommended answer.

Ask one question at a time using the `question` tool, waiting for my response before continuing. Never ask via plain text. Never bundle multiple questions into a single call — batching is bewildering.

Split facts from decisions. If a _fact_ can be found by exploring the environment (filesystem, tools, codebase), look it up yourself — don't ask. _Decisions_ (require my judgment or preference) are mine — put each to me and wait for my answer; never infer or answer on my behalf.

Do not proceed to create a plan until I confirm shared understanding has been reached. Treat my confirmation as a hard stop-gate before planning.
